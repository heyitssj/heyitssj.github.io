My Piano Journey

My Story:

Hi! My name is Sarah, and piano has been an essential part of my life since I was 11-12. I'm 16 now. When I first started taking piano lessons, I absolutely hated it. However, as I contnued to attend these classes every Saturday, I slowly began to fall in love with it. I have learned many things about myself since then, and I’ve continued to do so. If you haven’t learned this skill, I suggest you learn it. It doesn't matter if you consider yourself to be too "young" or too "old". There's something for everyone to learn. I guarantee that you’ll learn new things about yourself, and develop others. I promise that you won’t regret it.

Below are some songs that I love and some that I’ve learned to play:

Impromptu in A Flat, Op. 29 (Chopin)

Valse Melancolique (Rebikoff)

Moonlight Sonata (Beethoven)

Gipsy Rondo (Haydn)

Gitanerias (Lecuona)

Sonatina in C, Op. 20, No. 1 (Kuhlau)

Clair De Lune (Debussy)

Polonaise in G Minor (Chopin)
